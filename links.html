3dmm.com

WEBMASTER: Space Goat

The oldest 3dmm site around, first opened in 1997 under 'XSG Industries'. This site's still active today with a bustling community.
3dmm.co.uk

WEBMASTER: Will Maltby

First opening in 1999 under 'willsmovies.co.uk', this was one of the more popular 3dmm sites. It ran until 2002 whenever it shut down. In 2022, Will Cheyney revived the site as an archive.
3dmm.ca

WEBMASTER: Plopilpy

This site is the most canadian 3dmm website, ever. Started by plop in 2021 originally under '3dmm Dungeon', this site became pretty popular for what it's worth.
3dmm Studio

WEBMASTER: Will Cheyney

The second biggest UK 3dmm site (first being 3dmm.co.uk), launched in March 2001. After 3dmm.co.uk laid dormant for a few years, this site took over as the most popular UK 3dmm site.
3dmm.us

WEBMASTER: LT FILMS

This site was originally opened in 2022, but was left dormant for a while until december 2024 whenever it got relaunched.
Tnt Films

WEBMASTER: Pokerman

This site was launched in 2022 after pokerman got burnt out working on his previous site '3dmm Wasteland'. Even though it hasn't been updated in 2 years, it stil looks good.
Dragon Films

WEBMASTER: Greg Strnad

One of the more older 3dmm sites, launched in 1998. This site was fairly active with updates until 2006, and then in 2014 it became an archive of the past.
Benny's 3dmm Site

WEBMASTER: BurgerBenis

Opened in 2024 after his other site '3dmm.carrd.co' shut down, this is a pretty good site for a beginner. It has the basic necessities of a 3dmm site, but its simplistic design's amazing.

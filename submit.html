HOW TO SUBMIT MOVIES TO UNITED FRONT:


1. Make Your Movie (we accept .vmm or .3mm files for your movie).

2. If you have unused sounds (check below for example):



Click on the first option.

3. (FOR .VMM FILES) If there are any unused mods in the movie file, download Jayrod's VMM Cleaner to get rid of unused .vxp files in the film.

4. Pack the .vmm or .3mm file into a .zip file (with any custom fonts you used) and upload them to mediafire.

5. Whenever you have your file uploaded, send the file over to ltfilms2022@gmail.com with a thumbnail the dimensions of 544x306.

6. When your submission has been approved, you'll see it on the Movies Page.
